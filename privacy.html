Privacy Policy

Next Move Technologies, Inc. ("Next Move," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use our platform and website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information and only what is necessary to operate the platform and respond to your inquiries:

  • Email Signup Forms: Email address only. We collect emails when you sign up for updates or request platform information.
  • Demo Request Forms: Name, email, phone (optional), organization, and role. This information helps us respond to your request and schedule a demo.
  • Anonymized Usage Analytics: We track platform engagement using anonymized universally unique identifiers (UUIDs). We do not collect, store, or track personally identifiable information (PII). Analytics include: page views, feature interactions, time spent on platform, and completion rates. All data is associated only with a UUIDânot a name, account, or identity.
  • No User Accounts Required: The platform does not require logins, user accounts, or personal authentication. Players interact with the platform anonymously.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for limited, specific purposes:

  • Email Inquiries: To respond to signup requests, answer questions, and send relevant platform updates (only if you've opted in).
  • Demo Requests: To schedule and conduct product demonstrations with organizations interested in licensing the platform.
  • Platform Improvement: To analyze anonymized usage patterns and improve the platform's features, content, and user experience.
  • Evaluation and Research: To measure engagement and outcomes as part of our evaluation partnership with Ithaka S+R, using only aggregated, de-identified data.

We do not use your information for marketing purposes beyond educational outreach about the platform, and we never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

3. Information Sharing

We are committed to keeping your information private:

  • No Third-Party Sales: We do not sell your personal information to any third parties, period.
  • No Data Sharing with Marketing Partners: We do not share email addresses or contact information with marketing companies, data brokers, or commercial partners.
  • Institutional Partners: If your organization licenses the platform (Departments of Corrections, supervision agencies, community programs), we may share aggregated, de-identified usage data to report on platform engagement and outcomes. Personal information is never included.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request. We will make reasonable efforts to notify you of such requests unless prohibited by law.
  • Service Providers: We may engage third-party service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics) who process data only as instructed and are bound by confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Security

Next Move takes data security seriously. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure hosting on Amazon Web Services (AWS) with encryption at rest
  • Regular security reviews and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication protocols for internal systems
  • CJIS compliance standards for correctional system deployments

While we implement robust security practices, no system is completely secure. If you believe your information has been compromised, please contact us immediately at hello@nextmove.one.
