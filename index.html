The challenge

High-stakes transitions
deserve better preparation

Millions of people face life-defining transitions every year with no safe way to practice the decisions ahead. The consequences of being unprepared are devastating — for individuals, families, and communities.

1.9M

Reentry & ATI programs

People leaving incarceration and those in alternative-to-incarceration programs face an overwhelming cascade of decisions — housing, employment, supervision, benefits — with razor-thin margins for error. Launching in New York, expanding to all 50 states.

200K+

Veterans transitioning yearly

Nearly half of post-9/11 veterans report difficulty transitioning to civilian life — navigating benefits, employment, housing, and healthcare systems simultaneously.

20K+

Youth aging out of foster care

Every year, young people age out of the foster system with no safety net — facing housing, finances, healthcare, and legal decisions alone for the first time.

Millions

More transitions ahead

Domestic violence survivors rebuilding safety. People in recovery navigating sobriety. Families facing court systems for the first time. Every high-stakes transition deserves better preparation.

Our approach

Learn by doing, not by reading

Next Move uses interactive decision training — realistic branching scenarios where every choice has consequences. Players navigate the same decisions they'll face in real life, building judgment before the stakes are real.

🎯

Realistic Scenarios

Every decision node is grounded in real-world data — actual housing rules, benefit eligibility, court procedures, and employment barriers specific to each state and situation.

🌍

Four Languages

Built from day one in English, Spanish, French, and Haitian Creole — reaching communities that other platforms overlook entirely.

📱

Built for Access

Runs on any device — correctional tablets, phones, shared computers at shelters and reentry programs, VA resource centers, courthouses, and public libraries. No downloads, no accounts, no personal information collected.

🗺

State by State

Currently available for New York, with state-specific releases rolling out across all 50 states. Each version reflects that state's actual supervision rules, housing programs, employment laws, Medicaid status, and cost of living.

Traction & partnerships

Already in motion

Next Move isn't a concept — it's a product with institutional partners, an evidence-based evaluation, and a clear path to national scale.

CASES NYC — Phase 1 Launch Partner

One of the nation's oldest alternative-to-incarceration programs (founded 1967, 9,500+ diverted annually). Phase 1 pilot for reentry and ATI decision training.

Ithaka S+R — Evaluation Partner

200-participant quasi-experimental study over a 2-year timeline, measuring real-world outcomes including engagement, completion, knowledge gain, and recidivism reduction.

Distribution Reach

Built for the tablet platforms that already operate inside 6,270+ US correctional facilities — Securus, ViaPath, and Orijin — reaching incarcerated populations at scale.

BMCC — Authenticity Pipeline

Student developers with lived experience contribute to content development and authenticity review, ensuring every scenario reflects the real decisions people face.

How it works: Next Move is a B2B SaaS platform. Departments of Corrections and community supervision agencies license the platform annually on a per-facility basis. We never charge incarcerated individuals or their families — not for reentry, not for any module we build. Revenue comes from the institutions responsible for outcomes.
